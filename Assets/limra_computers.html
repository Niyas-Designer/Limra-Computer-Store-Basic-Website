How long does a general laptop repair take?

Basic hardware replacements (screen, keyboard, battery) or RAM/SSD upgrades are usually done within 1 to 2 hours. Advanced motherboard chip-level repair may take 1 to 3 days depending on component availability.

Do you install CCTV systems for commercial factories?

Yes, we specialize in CCTV installations for retail shops, homes, and large garment factories in Tiruppur. We handle IP cameras, DVR/NVR setups, and mobile viewing configuration.

Yes! We offer professional data recovery services for formatted, corrupted, or physically damaged Hard Disks, SSDs, and Pen drives. We charge only if data is successfully recovered.
